Google Snake: A Comprehensive Guide



Game Overview


Google Snake is a classic browser-based game developed by Google in 2003. The game was created as a way to showcase the capabilities of the HTML5 canvas element and the JavaScript programming language.



How to Play


To play Google Snake, simply open the game in your web browser. The objective of the game is to navigate your snake through a grid of cells, avoiding obstacles while collecting food pellets.




The game starts with a blank grid of cells, and you control your snake using the arrow keys or mouse.


As you move your snake, it will eat food pellets that appear on the screen. Each pellet contains energy points (EPs) that can be collected to grow longer.


Obstacles, represented by circles and lines, will try to block your path.





Tips and Tricks for Success


Here are some tips and tricks to help you succeed in Google Snake:




Move slowly at first, but as you gain speed, use it to your advantage by snapping your snake around obstacles.


Collect food pellets when they appear, but avoid eating them if possible. Eating a pellet will temporarily slow down your snake's movement.


Use the edges of the screen to change direction quickly and outmaneuver obstacles.





Why You Should Play It
